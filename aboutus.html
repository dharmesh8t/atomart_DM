Who We Are

AtomArt is a handmade fashion label born in the heart of Auroville. We fuse art and fashion to help you wear your identity—bold, raw, and unique.

Our Story

It started with a brush stroke and a blank t-shirt in Auroville. Dharmesh, our founder, believed that clothes should be more than just fabric—they should be stories. Today, AtomArt empowers artists and creators to express themselves on wearable canvases. Every shirt we make carries intention, rebellion, and creativity.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To turn self-expression into fashion by supporting raw, emerging artists and crafting clothes that speak louder than trends.

Our Vision

To become India's most loved indie fashion house where art and identity are stitched together.
